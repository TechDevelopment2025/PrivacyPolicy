# PrivacyPolicy
Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how reminder_reactNative (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

This draft is not legal advice, but it is written to cover the core disclosures expected for mobile apps, including account data, reminders, notifications, caregiver/family features, and optional microphone or file access.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:
Account information: such as your username, name, email address, password or authentication credentials, role, and linked caregiver or family account details.

Reminder and task information: such as reminders, schedules, routines, medication or health-related reminder labels, task status, shopping lists, notes, reports, and notification preferences.

Emergency or caregiver information: such as names, relationships, phone numbers, and other details you choose to add for family members, caregivers, or emergency contacts.

Notification information: such as push notification tokens, notification settings, reminder priority, acknowledgement status, and delivery preferences.

Voice or microphone data: if you choose to use voice-related features, the app may request microphone access to capture audio for that feature. We do not use microphone access for advertising or unrelated tracking.

Files or media: if you upload or attach files, images, or other content, we may collect and store the content you choose to provide.
Device and usage information: such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, and basic technical data needed to keep the app secure and working properly.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

Create and manage user accounts

Provide reminders, schedules, tasks, shopping lists, reports, and caregiver features

Send push notifications and reminder alerts

Sync information across devices or linked accounts

Personalize app settings and notification preferences

Provide customer support

Improve reliability, security, and app performance

Prevent fraud, misuse, or unauthorized access

Comply with legal obligations

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only in the following situations:

With service providers: We may use third-party providers for hosting, cloud storage, authentication, push notifications, analytics, diagnostics, or other app infrastructure. These providers may process information only as needed to provide services to us.
With linked users you choose: If the app lets you connect with caregivers, family members, or other users, information such as reminders, tasks, schedules, reports, and status updates may be visible to those linked users based on your app settings and role.
For legal or safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the public, or the app.

Business transfers: If the app or related business is transferred, merged, or sold, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy.

Push Notifications

The app may send reminders, alerts, and account-related notifications. You can manage notification permissions through your device settings and, where available, inside the app.

Permissions

The app may request permissions such as notifications, microphone access, file or media access, and internet access. These permissions are used only to support app features you use.

You can change permissions at any time in your device settings, but some features may not work correctly if permissions are disabled.
Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including secure transmission where appropriate. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain accounts, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

If you delete your account or request deletion, we will delete or de-identify your personal information unless we need to retain certain information for legal, security, backup, or legitimate operational reasons.

The app is not intended for children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Health and Reminder Information

The app may allow users to create health-related or medication-related reminders. The app is intended as an organizational reminder tool and is not a medical device or substitute for professional medical advice. Users are responsible for verifying medication, care, and health-related information with qualified professionals.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Those countries may have different data protection laws. Where required, we take steps to protect information consistent with applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice in the app or by other reasonable means.
